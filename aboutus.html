About Us

Founded in March 2000, TransitionPoint is a venture-backed consulting company focused on helping companies effectively use technology to meet strategic business goals. We have helped a number of clients successfully design, develop, and implement web-based applications for both internal corporate processes and customer-side presentation.

Management Team:

John Paul Ashenfelter, President and CTO
With over eight years experience in web application development, John Paul provides broad ranging analysis and development skills focusing on the rapid development of web-based business applications. His particular technical expertise lies in ColdFusion, Java, and database design and development with a strong focus on open-source software.

John Paul is a frequent speaker at conferences and a contributor to online resources such as the O'Reilly Network and the (now defunct) WebReview site. He has written three books on web databases and ColdFusion and has contributed or been a technical editor on a number of other books on the web, databases, and development.
